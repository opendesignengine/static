Sprint 1 Retrospective

What we did well?

  • Knocked out a lot of hardware stuff well
  • We planned a sprint that was well sized (the only not done PBIs were blocked by "the speed of government")
  • Kept things moving, this style of management is working for us
  • And the hardware work we are doing is getting done well

What didn't go well?

  • Way more paperwork to setup LLC in DC than OH, had no idea; still blocked on some of the paperwork
  • Could have done some of the PBIs a little faster (Bryan fought hardware more than expected and J had a hard time keeping up with pushing the paperwork sometimes)

What can we do better next time?

  • Make sure to fully process tutorials and documentation for new modules we are using
  • Be careful about taking on PBIs that play to our weaknesses, plan for longer times to complete when taking those PBIs
