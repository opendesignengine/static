Hey everyone,

I just finished putting together a first draft of the Initial Questions for review. Aaron, Greg, and Jeremy are already familiar with these questions. For Tim and Scott, these are questions we got from a presentation by Amanda Wozniak at an Open Hardware Summit a couple of years ago. We find that they make a great way to get small projects started with requirements analysis.

Usually we discuss these questions over the forums and in a meeting or two before getting them up in the wiki, but given the dialogs we have already had, Aaron and I thought we would see if I could get a straw man set of answers put together and see how close it was to what everyone was thinking.

Please take a look at the wiki page, and leave comments in this thread for changes/additions/deletions/approval.
