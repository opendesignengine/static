RE: v 1.0 Roadmap Evaluation of Navigation, Guidance, and Control Subsystem - Added by J. Simmons about 8 years ago

Following the conversation from last week's #EngineerSpeak, here are some thoughts on engineering expertise. GNC is a very controls and sensors heavy field. I am not really sure where our volunteers stand in terms of expertise in controls, but if we have controls experts, I doubt we have more than one or two, so I am going to make a conservative estimate and call this a 1 for now, with the understanding that I would be happy to revise this up if we have several controls folks and I just didn't realize it.

But, the potential for export control issues (as evidenced by the need to research where we would cross the line) probably puts this category out of the running for this round of development.
