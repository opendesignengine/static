Overview

This will be a portable device capable of controlling DMX devices, with transparency for intelligent fixtures. It's very much in planning stages as yet, but I've got a few talented individuals helping, and we should be able to put something cool together.

The ultimate goal is to design a piece hardware and accompanying software that can be used by someone in the field of entertainment lighting to test, control and play with conventional fixtures and dimmers, moving lights, RGB wash units and whatever else in the world speaks DMX, at a price point around $200.

This project is ambitious. I don't just want to create a great tool, I want to create an open-source one. I want the hardware and software to be available to anyone who cares to use them. I want to design an open-source format for fixture libraries, I want to put control of our equipment into the hands of the users. I want to change the way we look at our tools.
