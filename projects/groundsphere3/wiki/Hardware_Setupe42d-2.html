Tools¶

None.

Materials¶

  • (1) H-01: RTL-SDR Dongle
  • (2) H-02: 1/4 Wave Monpole Antenna
  • (3) H-03: TRAM NMO 5 1/2'' Magnet NMO Mounting with Rubber Boot

Safety Issues¶

  • Be careful of static discharge for the RTL-SDR dongle.
  • Antenna is very conductive, be careful of electric shock near high voltage sources.

Product¶

Full Hardware Assembly

Procedure¶

1. Prepare Mounting Slot¶

  1. Do a test fit (without glue) of the HSC-03: Controller Housing Base and HSC-10: Controller Housing Mounting Slot by sliding the Controller Housing Base into the Controller Housing Mounting Slot
  2. Separate the Controller Housing Base into the Controller Housing Mounting Slot
  3. Apply super glue to the inner perimeter of the Controller Housing Mounting Slot where the Controller Housing Base will sit, taking care to keep glue out of the cut out for the slot; apply extra super glue to the large raised surface to ensure a strong bond. See yellow lines in image below.
  4. Slide the Controller Housing Base into the Controller Housing Mounting Slot and press the pieces together per the glue's instructions (typically 1 minute)
  5. Allow the glue to cure per its instructions

  3. Attach HSC-09: Keyswitch to Controller Wing (note, the LED on the Keyswitch should be on the left side when the TRRS Wing is toward you)
