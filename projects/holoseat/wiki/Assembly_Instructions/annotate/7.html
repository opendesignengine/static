Below are the assembly instructions for the v0.2 *Holoseat* controller.  Remember, this is a breadboard level prototype.  While it is fully functional, the lack of permanent solder joints makes it unsuitable for long term use in the "real world".  If you want a permanent version, consider skipping the mini-breadboard and soldering the components directly onto the prototype board.
