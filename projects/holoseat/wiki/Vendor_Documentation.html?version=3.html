Speed & Direction Sensor TLE4966¶

The "TLE4966":Speed & Direction Sensor TLE4966 is the proposed sensor package for Holoseat. It is produced by Infineon. It comes in a number of form factors, listed below.

  • TLE4966V-1K
  • TLE4966K
  • TLE4966-2K
  • TLE4966-3K
  • TLE4966L
